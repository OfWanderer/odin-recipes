Sosyete Mantısı (Society Patty)

What is Sosyete Mantısı ?

As some of you might have guessed there actually is not an exact translation for the name of this Turkish food.Closest you can get is to call it in english probably would be a "Society Patty".It is basically a pastry filled with minced meat, topped with yogurt and sauce on it.

Now don't get scared!It does not contain any black magic, on the contrary it's a rather simple dish to make and enjoy yourself while eating.Unless you are a vegetarian, in that case I would suggest you to go look at other variety of patties or pastries that I can offer from my homeland.

Let's get to it!

Ingredients

  • 3 Phyllo Dough

For the Inside:

  • 300 Grams of Minced Meat

  • 1 Onion
    •  
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
    •  
  • ½ Teaspoon Sunflower Oil
    •  
  • Black Pepper
    •  
  • Chili Pepper
    •  
  • Salt
    •  
  • Parsley

To Wet the Phyllo Doughs:

  • ½ Water Glass of Water or a Water Glass of Yogurt mixed with ½ Water Glass of Water

  • 4 Tablespoons of Sunflower Oil

For The Topping:

  • Yogurt with Garlic

For the Sauce:

  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter

  • Mint
